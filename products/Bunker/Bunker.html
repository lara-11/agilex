The BUNKER tracked differential drive platform is a high-performance outdoor all-terrain AGV ready for the toughest challenges. Its two 650W brushless servo motors and six independent Christie suspension mechanism allows BUNKER to surmount obstacles 170mm in height including stairs and slopes up to 36°. Its 48V30Ah battery system provides 10km and speeds of 5km/h. The BUNKER also has a new battery upgrade to 48V60Ah and IP rating of IP54. BUNKER can carry payloads of 80kg and can turn in place. BUNKER interfaces with external devices using a CAN interface and C++ and ROS packages are available for further secondary development.

Optimized Drivetrain & Strong Suspension

Strong motors and a robust drivetrain give BUNKER the power to overcome significant obstacles in its path. Its Christie suspension, paired with shock absorbers provide stability to the platform and allows it to adapt to rough terrain. BUNKER’s forward tilt track design makes it able to climb steep slopes and tall steps. This touch design makes BUNKER proficient in handling difficult and complex tasks in the field.
