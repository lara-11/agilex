Meet The LIMO

The AgileX Robotics LIMO is a ROS development and learning platform that integrates four steering modes and comes pre-installed with plenty of demos and examples. LIMO is a perfect platform for robot education, research and development, and can serve as a model for industrial applications.

Supports

Robot Operating System

ROS support enables autonomous driving development, 3D SLAM & navigation as well as easy integration with additional sensors.

High-Quality Simulation

Gazebo offers the ability to accurately and efficiently simulate populations of robots in complex indoor and outdoor environments.

Obstacle Detection Avoidance & V-SLAM Technology

Development tools include RViz, Gazebo, and NoMachine. ROS nodes are provided for mapping and navigation. Using computer vision, it can detect certain objects like letters, numbers, and traffic lights. While navigating, it can sense obstacles in its way and plan local paths to avoid it while staying on track.

Open-Source Software

LIMO is a perfect platform for both beginners just learning robotics or advanced users. It is a great tool for education, or even industrial applications development like autonomous driving, computer vision, and AI. With plenty of open-source packages available onboard, the LIMO works great for engineers of all skill levels.

Four-wheel Differential Steering Mode

can tackle all complex terrain. Driving on any surface from smooth concrete to sandy roads is possible. This versatility allows developers to perform research on a wide range of applications.

Wheel Types

Ackermann Steering Mode is highly similar to steering a car, turning the inner and outer wheels at the appropriate angles for more stable steering. This system is customizable based on the steering speed.

Tracked Steering Mode uses wide contact with the ground and adjusts steering speeds of the left and right sides to traverse multiple surfaces such as smooth roads, sandy and rugged roads, slopes, etc.

Mecanum Wheel Steering Mode can perform translation and rotation in place in any direction, including straight, oblique, horizontal, S-shaped travel, zero-radius rotation, narrow and irregular spaces at any angle, etc.

NVIDIA® Jetson Nano™ Developer Kit

The onboard NVIDIA® Jetson Nano™ Developer Kit is a small, powerful computer that lets you run multiple neural networks in parallel for applications like image classification, object detection, segmentation, and speech processing. All in an easy-to-use platform that runs in as little as 5 watts.

ORBBEC® Dabai Stereo Depth Camera

The ORBBEC® Dabai Stereo Depth Camera is a high-precision, ultra-low power consumption and low-cost stereo depth camera that can provide color and depth images, and can be used to generate dense pointclouds. The ORBBEC® SDK and ROS wrappers are provided to make computer vision development a breeze. With a field of view up to 67.9°, a range between 0.3m and 3m, and an accuracy of +/-6mm, the Dabai is the perfect choice for the LIMO.

Simulation Table

LIMO comes with a specially designed simulation table. Fun and convenient, the simulation table can help you quickly simulate and test your model applications in the realistic scenarios.
