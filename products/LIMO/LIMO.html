LIMO is an innovative multi-modal, compact, and customizable mobile robot with AI modules and open-source ROS packages which enables education, researchers, enthusiasts to program and develop AI robots easier. The LIMO has four steering modes including Omni-directional steering, tracked steering, Ackermann and four-wheel differential, in line with strong perception sensors and the Nvidia Jetson Nano making it an excellent platform to develop indoor and outdoor applications while learning ROS. A beginner-friendly multi-functional table and rich ROS packages are provided allowing users to easily develop and validate new functions of AI and autonomous driving applications.


App Compatible

The LIMO app is a brand new feature implemented in this ROS technology that will replace the remote controller. Enabling it to be more flexible and inovative. This multi-modal motion control is bluetooth compatible and allows you to control all four-wheel differential modes including omni-directional steering, tracked steering, & ackermann. The strong perception sensors & Nvidia Jetson Nano also makes it perfect for indoor & outdoor usage.
